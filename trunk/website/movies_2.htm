  KALLU KONDORU PENNU  Woman of Stone
A Brief Synopsis

A bold film, attempting to look into the fall of the so called ‘Gulf-dream’. Told in the back ground of the Kuwait invasion by Iraq and the ensuing Gulf War in 1989, the film delves deeply into the hypocrisy and the small mindedness of a lower middleclass family as they foresee the possibility of the gulf-money source drying up.
